About

Revibe is a sustainable clothing pop-up that provides curated vintage and thrifted items for all humans, not associating clothing with gender. Items for sale include t-shirts, button ups, jackets, pants, jeans, hats, jewelry, shoes, and bags/purses.

Looking for something in particular? Reach out to me on Instagram (@back2thevibes) and let's find your vibe!
