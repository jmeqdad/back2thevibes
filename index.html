About

Revibe is a sustainable clothing pop-up that provides curated vintage and thrifted items for all, not associating style with gender. Items for sale include t-shirts, button ups, jackets, pants, jewelry, knickknacks, shoes, bags/purses, and much more!

You may be wondering how Revibe is sustainable. To start, we save clothes from going to the landfill, try to repopulate clothing, and use what is already made. We provide $5-$10 bins full of newer clothing as well as vintage clothes in an effort to provide a variety in pricing and make sure every piece finds a good home - not in the landfill. We work hard to thrift almost all of our business materials, such as hangers, tags, materials for bags, racks, and display pieces.

The mission for Revibe is to help people see that you can use what you already have and find your vibe/style without breaking the bank.

Looking for something in particular? Reach out to me on Instagram (@back2thevibes) and let's find your vibe!
